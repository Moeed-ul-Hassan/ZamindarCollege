Our Educators

Our faculty consists of highly qualified professionals with advanced degrees from prestigious institutions. Many have years of teaching experience, research accomplishments, and industry expertise that they bring to the classroom.

Faculty Development

At Zamindar College, we believe in the continuous professional development of our faculty members. We regularly organize workshops, seminars, and training sessions to keep our educators updated with the latest teaching methodologies, research trends, and technological advancements in education.

Our faculty members are encouraged to pursue research, present papers at conferences, and publish in academic journals. The college provides research grants and sabbatical leaves to support these scholarly activities.

We also have faculty exchange programs with other institutions, which allow our educators to gain exposure to different academic environments and teaching practices.
