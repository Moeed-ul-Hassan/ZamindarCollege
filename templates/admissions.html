Admission Requirements

Intermediate Programs

Eligibility Criteria
  • Passing certificate of Matriculation or equivalent examination with minimum 60% marks
  • For F.Sc Pre-Medical: At least 60% marks in Science subjects
  • For F.Sc Pre-Engineering: At least 60% marks in Science subjects with Mathematics
  • For F.Sc General Science: At least 55% marks in Science subjects
  • For F.A: At least 50% marks in relevant subjects
  • For I.Com: At least 50% marks in relevant subjects
Required Documents
  • Attested copy of Matriculation certificate and detailed marks certificate
  • Character certificate from the institution last attended
  • Four recent passport-sized photographs
  • Copy of CNIC or Form-B
  • Migration certificate (if applicable)

Bachelor's Programs

Eligibility Criteria
  • Passing certificate of Intermediate or equivalent examination with minimum 45% marks
  • For B.Sc: At least 45% marks in relevant science subjects
  • For B.A: At least 45% marks in relevant subjects
  • For B.Com: At least 45% marks in I.Com or equivalent
Required Documents
  • Attested copy of Intermediate certificate and detailed marks certificate
  • Attested copy of Matriculation certificate
  • Character certificate from the institution last attended
  • Four recent passport-sized photographs
  • Copy of CNIC
  • Migration certificate (if applicable)

Detailed Process:
  1. Obtain Application Form: Application forms can be obtained from the college Admissions Office or downloaded from the college website during the admission period.
  2. Complete Application: Fill out the application form completely and attach all required documents. Incomplete applications may be rejected.
  3. Submit Documents: Submit the completed application form along with required documents and application fee to the Admissions Office.
  4. Merit List: The college will display the merit list on the notice board and website. Selected candidates will be informed via SMS/email.
  5. Fee Payment: Selected candidates must pay the admission fee within the specified deadline to secure their admission.
  6. Orientation: Attend the orientation program to learn about college policies, facilities, and academic expectations.

Scholarships & Financial Aid

Zamindar College is committed to making quality education accessible to deserving students regardless of their financial background. We offer various scholarships and financial aid options to support our students.

Merit Scholarships

Based on academic performance in previous examinations.

  • 100% tuition fee waiver for students with 90%+ marks
  • 75% tuition fee waiver for students with 85-89% marks
  • 50% tuition fee waiver for students with 80-84% marks

Need-Based Financial Aid

Based on the financial circumstances of the student's family.

  • Tuition fee waivers ranging from 25% to 75%
  • Monthly stipends for extremely deserving students
  • Book and stationery allowance

Special Scholarships

For specific categories of students.

  • Sports scholarships for outstanding athletes
  • Scholarships for children of college staff
  • Scholarships for siblings (when two or more siblings are enrolled)
How to Apply for Scholarships
  1. Obtain the scholarship application form from the Financial Aid Office or download it from the college website.
  2. Submit the completed form along with all required documents by the specified deadline.
  3. For need-based financial aid, be prepared for an interview with the Scholarship Committee.
  4. Decisions regarding scholarships are made by the Scholarship Committee and are announced within two weeks after the application deadline.

Note: Continuation of scholarships is subject to maintaining specified academic performance and adherence to college rules and regulations.
