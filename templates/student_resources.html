Student Resources

Supporting your academic journey with comprehensive resources and services

Resources for Academic Success

At Zamindar College, we are committed to providing our students with the resources, facilities, and support they need to excel academically and grow personally. We believe that learning extends beyond the classroom, and we offer a wide range of resources to enrich your educational experience.

Whether you need academic support, career guidance, or personal development opportunities, we have resources designed to help you succeed in your educational journey and beyond.

Library & Learning Resources

The Zamindar College Library is the intellectual hub of our campus, offering a wealth of resources to support your academic pursuits.

Print Collection

Our library houses over 50,000 books covering a wide range of subjects, from sciences to humanities, along with journals, magazines, newspapers, and reference materials.

Digital Resources

Access our growing collection of e-books, online journals, and digital archives. Our digital library is accessible from anywhere on campus through our library computers.

Study Spaces

The library offers quiet individual study carrels, group study rooms, and comfortable reading areas to accommodate different learning preferences.

Research Assistance

Our professional librarians are available to help you find resources, navigate databases, and develop your research skills through one-on-one consultations and workshops.

Library Hours:
Monday to Friday: 8:00 AM - 8:00 PM
Saturday: 9:00 AM - 5:00 PM
Sunday: Closed

Computer & Technology Resources

Zamindar College provides state-of-the-art computing facilities to support your academic and research needs.

Computer Labs

Our three computer labs are equipped with the latest hardware and software, providing students with hands-on experience in computer applications and programming.

Campus Wi-Fi

Secure Wi-Fi coverage is available throughout the campus, allowing students to access online resources and collaborate on academic projects from anywhere on college grounds.

Printing & Scanning

Printing and scanning facilities are available in the computer labs and library. Students receive a printing quota each semester for academic work.

Specialized Software

Access to specialized software for different disciplines, including statistical analysis tools, programming environments, design software, and more.

Computer Lab Hours:
Monday to Friday: 8:00 AM - 6:00 PM
Saturday: 9:00 AM - 3:00 PM
Sunday: Closed

Student Life & Wellness Resources

Student Activities & Organizations

Financial Resources

Financial Support Options
