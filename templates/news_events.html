September 10, 2023

Annual Convocation Ceremony 2023

Zamindar College held its Annual Convocation Ceremony on September 5, 2023, at the college auditorium. Over 500 graduates received their degrees in various disciplines. The ceremony was presided over by the Honorable Minister of Education, who lauded the college's contribution to higher education in the region.

The Principal, Dr. Abdul Hameed, congratulated the graduates and emphasized the importance of lifelong learning and ethical leadership in his address. Gold medals and special awards were presented to outstanding students who excelled in academics and extracurricular activities.

The ceremony was attended by distinguished guests, faculty members, students, and parents, making it a memorable event for the Zamindar College community.

August 25, 2023

Faculty Research Published in International Journal

Dr. Samina Ahmed, Head of the Arts Department, recently published her research paper titled "Contemporary Trends in Pakistani Literature" in the International Journal of South Asian Studies. The research explores the evolution of literary themes in Pakistani literature over the past two decades and their reflection of socio-political changes in the country.

This publication adds to the growing list of international research contributions by Zamindar College faculty, enhancing the institution's academic reputation globally.

August 18, 2023

MOU Signed with University of Lahore

Zamindar College has signed a Memorandum of Understanding (MOU) with the University of Lahore to facilitate academic collaboration, faculty exchange, and joint research projects. The agreement also includes provisions for student transfer and joint academic programs.

This partnership will create new opportunities for students and faculty members, allowing them to benefit from the resources and expertise of both institutions. Special workshops and seminars will be organized under this collaboration.

August 15, 2023

Annual Sports Day Celebrations

The college's Annual Sports Day was celebrated with great enthusiasm on August 12, 2023. Students participated in various athletic competitions and team sports, showcasing their physical prowess and sporting spirit.

The event featured track and field events, cricket, football, basketball, volleyball, and indoor games. Medals and trophies were awarded to outstanding athletes and winning teams. The Science Department emerged as the overall champion.

July 10, 2023

Science Exhibition 2023

Students showcased their innovative science projects at the annual Science Exhibition held on July 8, 2023. The event was attended by prominent scientists, educators, and community members who were impressed by the creativity and scientific rigor of the projects.

Projects ranged from renewable energy solutions and environmental conservation to robotics and artificial intelligence applications. Several projects received recognition for their innovative approach to addressing real-world problems.

Student Achievements

National Science Competition Winner

Congratulations to Fahad Ali, a final year student of B.Sc Physics, for winning the first prize in the National Science Competition 2023 held at Islamabad. His project on "Low-Cost Solar Energy Solutions for Rural Areas" was highly appreciated by the judges for its innovation and practical applicability.

Fahad's achievement brings honor to Zamindar College and highlights the quality of scientific education and research at our institution. He received a cash prize of Rs. 100,000 and an opportunity to present his work at an international conference.

August 5, 2023

Inter-Collegiate Debate Champions

Zamindar College's debate team emerged as champions in the Inter-Collegiate Debate Competition held at Government College University, Lahore. Our team, consisting of Ayesha Khan (B.A English), Hassan Ahmed (B.Sc), and Sana Malik (I.Com), impressed the judges with their articulate arguments and critical thinking.

Ayesha Khan was also awarded the "Best Speaker" title for her outstanding performance throughout the competition. This victory is a testament to our students' communication skills and intellectual capabilities.

July 15, 2023

Cricket Team Wins Regional Championship

Our college cricket team has won the Regional Inter-Collegiate Cricket Championship by defeating Punjab College in the final match. The team showcased exceptional skills and sportsmanship throughout the tournament.

Team captain Usman Khan (B.Com) was named "Player of the Tournament" for his outstanding all-round performance. The victory has qualified our team for the Provincial Championship scheduled for October 2023.
