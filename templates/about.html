About Zamindar College

Learn about our rich history, mission, and vision

Our History

Zamindar College, Gujrat was established in 1938 as a premier educational institution in Pakistan. The college was founded with the vision of providing quality education to the youth of the region, particularly those from rural backgrounds who had limited access to higher education.

The college was established through the generous donation of land and initial funding by local zamindars (landowners) who recognized the importance of education for the development of the region. This is how the college got its name "Zamindar College".

Over the past eight decades, Zamindar College has grown significantly in terms of infrastructure, academic programs, and student enrollment. From a modest beginning with a few departments, the college now offers a wide range of academic programs in science, arts, and commerce at both intermediate and degree levels.

The college has produced numerous distinguished alumni who have made significant contributions in various fields including politics, civil service, medicine, engineering, education, and business. The alumni of Zamindar College continue to bring honor to their alma mater through their achievements and service to society.

Throughout its history, Zamindar College has maintained its commitment to academic excellence, character building, and service to the community. The college continues to evolve and adapt to the changing educational landscape while preserving its core values and traditions.

Our Mission

The mission of Zamindar College is to provide high-quality education that nurtures intellectual growth, fosters critical thinking, and builds character. We are committed to creating an inclusive learning environment that empowers students to realize their full potential and become responsible citizens who contribute positively to society.

Our Vision

Our vision is to be recognized as a leading educational institution that sets high standards of academic excellence, innovation, and ethical leadership. We aspire to produce graduates who are not only knowledgeable in their fields but also possess the skills, values, and mindset needed to address the challenges of an ever-changing world.

Our Core Values

We uphold the highest standards of honesty, fairness, and ethical conduct in all our actions and interactions. We value transparency and accountability.

Respect & Diversity

We embrace diversity and foster an inclusive environment where all individuals are respected and valued. We celebrate different perspectives and experiences.

Innovation & Creativity

We encourage innovative thinking and creative problem-solving. We adapt to changing times while maintaining our core values and traditions.

We are committed to serving our community and making positive contributions to society. We instill in our students a sense of social responsibility.

Campus Facilities

Library

Our well-stocked library houses over 50,000 books, journals, and digital resources. It provides a quiet and conducive environment for reading and research.

Science Laboratories

We have state-of-the-art laboratories for Physics, Chemistry, and Biology equipped with modern apparatus to facilitate hands-on learning and practical experiments.

Computer Labs

Our computer labs are equipped with the latest hardware and software, providing students with hands-on experience in computer applications and programming.

Sports Facilities

We have excellent sports facilities including a cricket ground, football field, basketball court, and indoor games area to promote physical fitness and sportsmanship.

Auditorium

Our modern auditorium can accommodate up to 500 people and is used for college functions, seminars, conferences, and cultural events.

Cafeteria

Our cafeteria provides nutritious and affordable meals in a comfortable environment where students can relax and socialize during breaks.

Accreditation & Affiliations

Zamindar College is recognized by the Higher Education Commission (HEC) of Pakistan and is affiliated with the University of Gujrat. Our academic programs meet the quality standards set by these regulatory bodies.

The college also has academic collaborations with several national and international institutions to provide exchange programs, research opportunities, and workshops for students and faculty members.
