Academic Programs

Discover our diverse range of academic offerings designed to prepare you for success

Excellence in Education

At Zamindar College, we offer a comprehensive range of academic programs at both intermediate and degree levels. Our curriculum is designed to provide students with a strong foundation in their chosen fields while developing critical thinking, problem-solving abilities, and practical skills.

Our academic programs are regularly updated to reflect current developments in various disciplines and to meet the evolving demands of the job market and higher education institutions.

Intermediate Programs (F.Sc, F.A, I.Com)

F.Sc Programs

Our Faculty of Science (F.Sc) programs prepare students for careers in medicine, engineering, and other scientific fields. We offer the following specializations:

F.Sc Pre-Medical

Subjects: Physics, Chemistry, Biology, English, Urdu, Islamic Studies/Ethics

Ideal for students aspiring to pursue careers in medicine, dentistry, pharmacy, and allied health sciences.

F.Sc Pre-Engineering

Subjects: Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics, English, Urdu, Islamic Studies/Ethics

Perfect for students interested in engineering, architecture, computer science, and other technical fields.

F.Sc General Science

Subjects: Physics, Mathematics, Computer Science/Statistics, English, Urdu, Islamic Studies/Ethics

Suitable for students interested in computer science, information technology, statistics, and mathematics-related disciplines.

F.A Programs

Our Faculty of Arts (F.A) programs provide a strong foundation in humanities and social sciences. We offer the following subject combinations:

F.A with Economics

Major Subjects: Economics, Psychology/Sociology, Education/Statistics

Compulsory Subjects: English, Urdu, Islamic Studies/Ethics, Pakistan Studies

F.A with Literature

Major Subjects: Urdu/English Literature, History, Education/Philosophy

Compulsory Subjects: English, Urdu, Islamic Studies/Ethics, Pakistan Studies

F.A General

Choose any three elective subjects from: Economics, Psychology, Sociology, Education, Statistics, History, Geography, Civics, Islamic Studies

Compulsory Subjects: English, Urdu, Islamic Studies/Ethics, Pakistan Studies

I.Com Programs

Our Intermediate in Commerce (I.Com) program is designed for students interested in business, finance, and commerce. The program covers:

I.Com

Subjects: Principles of Accounting, Principles of Economics, Principles of Commerce, Business Mathematics & Statistics, Banking, Computer Science

Compulsory Subjects: English, Urdu, Islamic Studies/Ethics

This program provides a solid foundation for students planning to pursue higher education in business administration, commerce, accounting, finance, or marketing.

Bachelor's Degree Programs (B.A, B.Sc, B.Com)

B.Sc Programs

Our Bachelor of Science (B.Sc) programs offer specialized education in various scientific disciplines. Students can choose from the following subject combinations:

B.Sc Physical Sciences

Major Subjects (choose two): Physics, Mathematics, Chemistry, Computer Science, Statistics

Compulsory Subjects: English, Islamic Studies/Ethics, Pakistan Studies

B.Sc Biological Sciences

Major Subjects: Zoology, Botany, Chemistry

Compulsory Subjects: English, Islamic Studies/Ethics, Pakistan Studies

B.Sc Mathematics

Major Subjects: Mathematics, Physics, Statistics/Computer Science

Compulsory Subjects: English, Islamic Studies/Ethics, Pakistan Studies

B.A Programs

Our Bachelor of Arts (B.A) programs provide comprehensive education in humanities and social sciences. Students can choose from various subject combinations, including:

B.A with Economics

Major Subjects: Economics, Statistics/Mathematics, Political Science/Sociology

Compulsory Subjects: English, Islamic Studies/Ethics, Pakistan Studies

B.A with Literature

Major Subjects: English Literature, Urdu Literature, History/Philosophy

Compulsory Subjects: English, Islamic Studies/Ethics, Pakistan Studies

B.A General

Choose any two major subjects from: Economics, Psychology, Sociology, Political Science, History, Geography, Islamic Studies, Education, Philosophy, Mathematics, Statistics

Compulsory Subjects: English, Islamic Studies/Ethics, Pakistan Studies

B.Com Program

Our Bachelor of Commerce (B.Com) program provides comprehensive education in business and commerce. The program covers:

B.Com

Major Subjects: Financial Accounting, Cost Accounting, Business Taxation, Business Law, Economics, Banking & Finance, Business Mathematics & Statistics, Marketing, Management

Compulsory Subjects: English, Islamic Studies/Ethics, Pakistan Studies

This program prepares students for careers in accounting, finance, banking, marketing, and management, or for pursuing higher education in business-related fields.

Academic Features

Experienced Faculty

Our programs are taught by highly qualified faculty members with extensive teaching experience and expertise in their respective fields.

Well-Equipped Laboratories

Our science programs are supported by modern laboratories that provide students with hands-on experience and practical skills.

Computer Labs

Students have access to well-equipped computer labs with internet connectivity for research and computer-based learning.

Career Counseling

We provide career counseling services to help students make informed decisions about their academic and career paths.

Scholarships

We offer merit-based scholarships to recognize and reward academic excellence and support deserving students.
