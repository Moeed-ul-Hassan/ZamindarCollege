Administration Block

The central hub for all administrative functions, including admissions office, accounts department, and principal's office. The historic building dates back to 1925 and features colonial architecture.

Central Library

Our state-of-the-art library houses over 50,000 books, journals, and digital resources. It features spacious reading halls, discussion rooms, and computer terminals for research purposes.

Science Block

Home to our Physics, Chemistry, Biology, and Computer Science departments. The building contains modern laboratories equipped with the latest scientific instruments and technology.

Arts Block

This building houses the Humanities and Social Sciences departments, including English, Economics, and Sociology. Features lecture halls, seminar rooms, and a small auditorium.

Sports Complex

Our comprehensive sports facilities include a cricket ground, football field, basketball and tennis courts, and an indoor gym. The complex also houses a swimming pool and athletics track.

Student Hostels

Comfortable and secure accommodation for out-of-town students. Separate hostels for male and female students with modern amenities, study rooms, and recreational facilities.

Campus Cafeteria

A spacious dining facility offering nutritious and affordable meals throughout the day. The cafeteria also includes a coffee shop and outdoor seating area for students to relax and socialize.

Student Life

Vibrant Campus Community

Diverse and Inclusive Community

Zamindar College is home to a diverse student body from various cultural, social, and economic backgrounds. Our inclusive environment celebrates this diversity and provides equal opportunities for all students to grow and excel.

Campus life at Zamindar College extends beyond academics, offering numerous opportunities for personal growth, leadership development, and social engagement. From participating in clubs and societies to contributing to community service projects, students can explore their interests and develop important life skills.

"My experience at Zamindar College has been transformative. The supportive community, excellent faculty, and numerous extra-curricular opportunities have helped me grow personally and academically."

- Hasan Ali, BS Physics, Final Year

Hostel Life

Comfortable & Secure Accommodation

Our hostels provide a comfortable living environment for students who come from different cities and regions. The hostel facilities are designed to create a conducive atmosphere for both academic pursuits and social interaction.

Hostel Features:

  • Furnished rooms with study tables and storage space

  • Three nutritious meals daily

Sports & Recreation

Zamindar College has a strong tradition of excellence in sports. We believe that physical fitness and competitive sports are essential components of a well-rounded education. Our college regularly participates in inter-collegiate, regional, and national tournaments in various sports disciplines.

Cricket Ground

Well-maintained cricket pitch with practice nets and pavilion for both practice and competitive matches.

Football Field

Regulation-size football field with proper marking and goal posts for training and competitions.

Basketball Courts

Multiple basketball courts with standard equipment for practice and tournaments.

Indoor Sports Complex

Facilities for badminton, table tennis, chess, and other indoor games and sports.

Athletic Track

400-meter running track for athletics practices and competitions.

Fitness Center

Modern gym equipped with cardio and strength training equipment for students and staff.

Campus Safety & Security

Your Safety is Our Priority

Zamindar College is committed to providing a safe and secure environment for all students, faculty, and staff. Our comprehensive security measures ensure that everyone on campus can focus on their studies and activities without concerns about their safety.

CCTV Surveillance

The entire campus is monitored through CCTV cameras positioned at strategic locations.

Security Personnel

Trained security guards patrol the campus 24/7 to ensure safety at all times.

ID Card System

Mandatory ID cards for all students and staff to control access to campus facilities.

Fire Safety

Regular fire drills and well-maintained fire safety equipment throughout the campus.
