Welcome to Zamindar College

Zamindar College, Gujrat, established in 1938, is one of the premier educational institutions in Pakistan. With a rich history spanning over eight decades, we have been at the forefront of academic excellence, producing graduates who have excelled in various fields.

Our mission is to provide quality education that nurtures intellectual growth, fosters critical thinking, and builds character. We strive to create an environment that encourages creativity, innovation, and ethical leadership.

From the Principal's Desk

Dear Students and Faculty,

It gives me immense pleasure to present the latest edition of "Shaheen", our college literary magazine. This publication showcases the creative talents, poetry, essays, and "Tahreer" (creative writing) of our talented students.

The past academic year has been exceptional in many ways. Our BS program, affiliated with the University of Gujrat, has shown remarkable growth. Our students have excelled in academics, literary pursuits, and cultural activities, bringing laurels to the institution.

I congratulate the editorial team for their dedication and hard work in bringing out this wonderful publication.

Prof. Ihsaan ul Haq
Principal, Zamindar College
