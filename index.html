Welcome to Zamindar College

Zamindar College, Gujrat, established in 1938, is one of the premier educational institutions in Pakistan. With a rich history spanning over eight decades, we have been at the forefront of academic excellence, producing graduates who have excelled in various fields.

Our mission is to provide quality education that nurtures intellectual growth, fosters critical thinking, and builds character. We strive to create an environment that encourages creativity, innovation, and ethical leadership.

From the Principal's Desk

Dear Students and Faculty,

It gives me immense pleasure to present the Spring 2023 edition of our college magazine. This publication reflects the creative talents and intellectual achievements of our students and faculty.

The past academic year has been exceptional in many ways. Our students have excelled in academics, sports, and cultural activities, bringing laurels to the institution.

I congratulate the editorial team for their dedication and hard work in bringing out this wonderful publication.

Principal
Zamindar College

Academic Achievements

Science Olympiad Winners

Our students secured top positions in the National Science Olympiad held in March 2023. Ahsan Khan (BSc Physics) won the gold medal in the physics category.

Research Publications

Dr. Farooq Ahmed from the Chemistry Department published his research in the International Journal of Chemical Sciences. His work on sustainable chemical processes received significant recognition.

Sports Highlights

Cricket Championship

Zamindar College Cricket Team won the Inter-College Cricket Championship for the third consecutive year, defeating Government College in the finals.

Athletics Meet

Our athletes performed exceptionally well in the District Athletics Meet, winning 5 gold, 3 silver, and 4 bronze medals in various track and field events.
