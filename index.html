Welcome to Zamindar College

Zamindar College, Gujrat, established in 1938, is one of the premier educational institutions in Pakistan. With a rich history spanning over eight decades, we have been at the forefront of academic excellence, producing graduates who have excelled in various fields.

Our mission is to provide quality education that nurtures intellectual growth, fosters critical thinking, and builds character. We strive to create an environment that encourages creativity, innovation, and ethical leadership.

1

From the Principal's Desk

Dear Students and Faculty,

It is a matter of immense pleasure for me to write a few words for "The Shaheen" magazine of Zamindar College Gujrat. This magazine is a platform for our students to express their creative thoughts and showcase their literary talents through poetry, essays, and creative writing (Tahreer).

I congratulate the editor and his team for their tireless efforts in bringing out this publication which highlights the achievements of our college and its students.

I hope this magazine will serve as an inspiration for future students and contribute to the rich literary tradition of our institution.

Prof. Ihsaan ul Haq
Principal, Zamindar College

2

Editorial

The entire Editorial Board takes great pride in presenting the Annual Issue of "The Shaheen" 2019-20 to our readers. This magazine serves as a mirror reflecting the accomplishments and creative voices of our students.

In this issue, we have included a diverse range of content including Urdu and English poetry, essays, articles, and creative pieces. We have made a conscious effort to include works that represent the depth of thought and creativity present in our student body.

We are grateful to the college administration for their unwavering support and guidance throughout the process of putting together this magazine. We also thank all contributors who have enriched this publication with their thoughtful and insightful creations.

Dr. Muhammad Anwar
Chief Editor

Dreams and Reality

I dreamt of a world so bright and fair,
Where love and peace were everywhere.
But woke to a reality harsh and cold,

حرفِ تمنا

میں نے چاہا تھا کہ دنیا میں منور ہو جاؤں
زندگی کے سفر میں ایک مشعل بن جاؤں
ظلمتوں میں رکھے سب کے لیے روشن تارے
ہر طرف علم و ہنر کا سا چمکتا سورج بن جاؤں

زندگی اک امتحاں ہے، میں اس سے ڈرتا نہیں
سیکھنے کی راہ میں، کبھی ہمت ہارتا نہیں
ہوں قدم آگے بڑھاتا، مجھے منزل کی تلاش
زمینداریوں کی شمع پہ پروانہ بن جاؤں

- علی رضا، بی ایس اردو

The Digital Revolution

The 21st century has witnessed an unprecedented digital revolution that has transformed every aspect of human life. From communication to education, healthcare to entertainment, the impact of digital technology is profound and far-reaching.

As students of this digital age, we must embrace technology while maintaining our critical thinking skills. We must learn to navigate the vast ocean of information available at our fingertips, distinguishing fact from fiction, and using digital tools to enhance our learning experience.

At Zamindar College, we are fortunate to have access to modern computer labs and digital resources that prepare us for the challenges of this digital era. The skills we acquire today will serve as the foundation for our future success in an increasingly digitized world.

- Fatima Batool, BS Computer Science

The Importance of Education in Modern Society

Education is the cornerstone of human progress and societal development. In today's rapidly evolving world, education has become more crucial than ever before. It not only equips individuals with knowledge and skills but also shapes their character and worldview.

At Zamindar College, we believe that quality education should be accessible to all. Our institution has been at the forefront of providing excellent educational opportunities to students from diverse backgrounds since 1938. We take pride in our legacy of academic excellence and our commitment to nurturing responsible citizens for Pakistan.

The BS programs affiliated with University of Gujrat have opened new avenues for higher education in our region. These programs offer specialized knowledge in various disciplines, preparing students for the challenges of the professional world and fostering a culture of research and innovation.

- Dr. Adnan Malik, Professor of Sociology

Student Activities

Literary Society

The Literary Society of Zamindar College has been exceptionally active this year, organizing poetry recitations, debates, and essay competitions. The annual Literary Festival held in November 2019 was a grand success, with participation from colleges across Punjab.

The society also published monthly newsletters featuring student contributions, providing a regular platform for creative expression. The "Words of Wisdom" corner featuring quotes and reflections became particularly popular among students.

Science Club

The Science Club organized several exhibitions and workshops throughout the academic year. The highlight was the Innovation Fair where students presented their science projects and prototypes. The robotics workshop conducted in collaboration with University of Gujrat received overwhelming response.

The club's initiative to organize educational trips to scientific institutions exposed students to practical applications of theoretical concepts and inspired many to pursue careers in scientific research.
