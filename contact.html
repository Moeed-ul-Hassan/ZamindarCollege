Frequently Asked Questions

What are the visiting hours for campus tours?

Campus tours are conducted Monday through Friday from 9:00 AM to 2:00 PM. It is recommended to schedule a tour in advance by contacting the Admissions Office at +92-55-9200273 or emailing admissions@zamindarcollege.edu.pk.

How can I request official transcripts?

To request official transcripts, current and former students need to submit a written request to the Examination Office along with the required fee. The request should include your full name, enrollment number, program of study, years attended, and the purpose of the transcript request. Processing typically takes 5-7 working days.

How do I apply for a position at Zamindar College?

Job openings at Zamindar College are posted on our website under the "Careers" section. To apply, submit your resume, cover letter, and other required documents to careers@zamindarcollege.edu.pk or in person at the HR Office. Be sure to specify the position you are applying for in the subject line or on the envelope.

How can I reserve college facilities for an event?

To reserve college facilities such as the auditorium, sports fields, or meeting rooms, contact the Administration Office at admin@zamindarcollege.edu.pk or +92-55-9200277. Provide details about the event including the date, time, expected number of attendees, and specific facility requirements. Reservation requests should be made at least two weeks in advance.
