Our Departments

Physics Department

Our Physics Department is equipped with state-of-the-art laboratories and experienced faculty members specializing in various fields of physics.

  • Advanced laboratory facilities
  • Research opportunities for students
  • Regular seminars and workshops

Chemistry Department

The Chemistry Department offers comprehensive education in organic, inorganic, and physical chemistry with hands-on laboratory experience.

  • Well-equipped organic and inorganic labs
  • Industry partnerships for practical exposure
  • Research projects in various specializations

Mathematics Department

Our Mathematics Department fosters analytical thinking and problem-solving skills with a blend of theoretical and applied mathematics.

  • Modern computational facilities
  • Mathematics competitions and clubs
  • Strong focus on applied mathematics
